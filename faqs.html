  1. How can I be certain my order's been received?

    2. We’ll send a confirmation message to the number you used in your booking within the day. Orders for the next day that were made after 6pm will get their confirmation in the morning of the next day.

  2. Can I order from your website anytime?

    3. Sure you can! But your order will get confirmed only between 9:30am to 6:00pm, so try to book within that time frame to get instant confirmation.

  3. Can I make changes to my order after it has been confirmed?

    4. Changes can’t be made once order is confirmed and payment is done. If you are unhappy with your order, kindly give us a call at 09178120286 between 10:00am to 6:00pm or leave us a text message. We address these on a case-by-case basis but we will try our best to work towards a satisfactory solution.

  4. How will my orders get delivered?

    5. Don’t worry! You don’t have to do anything! Once your order is ready for pick up, our partner for deliveries, Mr. Speedy, will automatically assign a driver to deliver the food to you.

  5. How far can you deliver?

    6. To give our customers the full Yedang Experience, we have limited our deliveries to up to 28km. But let’s say you're more than 28km away and you’d still like to make an order, send us a text message at 09178120286 and maybe we can work things out.
