About us

In September 2020, Eden Pearl T. San Antonio cut the ribbon of Mommy Puring Variety Store, a humble store located at the heart of the public market of Quezon, Nueva Ecija. Initially, she looked forward to establishing a grocery store that would sell goods that cater to the essential commodities of the townsfolk. However, as someone who passionately earned a degree in Marketing, she desired to use her learnings for vending products that would be both unique and can earn customers' hearts.

Following this premise, she decided to market Korean treats and goodies. The business started with an initial capital of PHP 10,000.00, exclusively worth of Korean goods and treats. A year after, the business flourished and became able to sell bulk amounts of Korean treats and goodies. At present, the business continues to offer Korean treats and goodies.
