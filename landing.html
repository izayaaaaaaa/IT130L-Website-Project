About Us

Eden San Antonio started Mommy Puring, a humble store located at Quezon, Nueva Ecija. She initially envisioned a grocery store that would sell essential commodities. However, as someone who finished Marketing in College, she desired to use her learnings for vending products that would be both unique and can earn customers' hearts.
